Programmes

Four age bands, one journey

Boys to Men Mentorship Camp serves boys and young men from 8 to 23, grouped so every session speaks to where a boy actually is, not just how old he is.

Age BandNameFocus
23–18Young MenLife readiness, independence, and deploying what has been developed
17–14Rising MenIdentity, confidence, and early leadership development
13–11Path FindersBuilding the foundation between discovery and development
10–8Young ExplorersEarly self-discovery and self-awareness

MEP Masterclass continues this same curriculum beyond age 23. Girls Rising serves the equivalent younger female audience under Potential Unexplored Ltd's Life Readiness Programme.

What a Camp Day Looks Like

Structured, active, and reflective

Mentorship Conversations

Small-group and one-on-one time with mentors built around trust, not lecture.

Leadership Activities

Team challenges, games, and role play used as diagnostic and teaching tools.

Life Skills Training

Practical sessions on communication, decision making, and responsibility.

Reflection Sessions

Guided time for boys to process what they are discovering about themselves.

Entrepreneurship Exposure

Early exposure to practical, real-world thinking about work and value creation.

Team Challenges

Physical and mental challenges that build resilience and cooperation.
