Partner With Us

Boys to Men grows through partnership

Whether you want to mentor directly, support financially, or bring this programme to your own community or country, there is a clear path in.

Mentor a boy through one of the most formative stages of his life

We are always looking for men of good character who are willing to be vetted, trained, and consistently present, both during camp and in the follow-up months after. This is not a one-off appearance, it is a real commitment to a small group of boys.

Fund a boy's place, or fund the movement

Camp fees are not within reach for every family who needs this programme. Donor and sponsor support directly funds places for boys who could not otherwise attend, as well as the after-camp follow-up that makes the transformation last.

Bring Boys to Men into your own context

The Discover, Develop, Deploy methodology behind this programme is built to be replicable. Institutions, schools, and organisations, in Uganda, elsewhere in Africa, or internationally, can invite Boys to Men to deliver, co-deliver, or train local facilitators in the same approach.
