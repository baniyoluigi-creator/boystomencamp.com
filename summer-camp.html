Intakes · Summer Camp

Boys to Men Summer Camp

A concentrated, immersive version of the full Boys to Men experience, built for a boy who may only have a few weeks at home each year.

9 Days · Kampala Only · Ages 8–23

Next Summer Camp: July 7–16, 2027
Location: Kampala only
Fee: UGX 990,000 or USD 250, covering the full 9-day camp.

Diaspora-specific logistics, travel guidance, and mentor follow-up continue after your son returns abroad. Boys are clustered by age automatically on arrival, so every activity fits where your son actually is.

Apply for the Summer Camp

Built For Diaspora Families

Why this camp exists

Raising a son abroad often means raising him without the extended family, culture, and rites of passage that shaped previous generations. The Summer Camp gives him a genuine, structured reconnection to Uganda.

Read more for diaspora parents

Boys to Men Mentorship Camp is a flagship youth development initiative of Potential Unexplored Ltd, building bridges to manhood across Uganda and Africa.
