Building Bridges to Manhood in Africa

Every boy carries potential. We help him discover, develop, and deploy it.

Boys to Men Mentorship Camp is a mentorship and life-readiness experience for boys and young men aged 8 to 23, rooted in Uganda and open to families across Africa and the diaspora.

The Boys to Men Journey

From potential to progress

Every camp, every mentoring conversation, and every follow-up session moves a boy through three stages.

1

Discover

Who he is: identity, purpose, self-awareness, and the potential already within him.

2

Develop

Who he can become: personal mastery, practical mastery, and people mastery.

3

Deploy

What he does with it: leading himself, leading others, and creating real impact.

Why Boys Need This

Preparing boys for a changing world

Many boys are growing up without positive male role models, safe spaces to be honest about who they are, or guidance through the specific transitions that shape manhood. Boys to Men exists to build that bridge, wherever a family calls home.

From the Founder

Built by someone who has walked this road with hundreds of teams

Banio Luiji Nobert is the Founder of Boys to Men Mentorship Camp and Team Leader of Potential Unexplored Ltd, a leadership development and life readiness consultancy working across Uganda and Africa. He has spent over a decade training, coaching, and mentoring individuals and teams, with a client portfolio spanning UNHCR, Bank of Uganda, Centenary Bank, Mastercard Foundation, and more than fifty institutions across the continent.
