Start the application

This is the first real step toward camp. It takes a few minutes, and our team will follow up personally with next steps.

Kampala, Arua, and Gulu each run all four age clusters, every December and January.

9 days, July 7–16, 2027, Kampala only, built specifically for diaspora families.

Boys are placed into age-appropriate clusters automatically once they arrive, Young Explorers, Path Finders, Rising Men, or Young Men, based on age. There is nothing to choose here.
Local intake fee: contact us for current pricing by region.

By submitting, you agree to be contacted by the Boys to Men team about this application.

What happens after you apply

1. Our team reviews your application within a few working days.

2. We confirm intake dates, location, and cost.

3. You complete registration and receive a full preparation guide.

A calm, coaching-based approach

Boys to Men is not a disciplinary camp. Read our full Safeguarding commitment.
