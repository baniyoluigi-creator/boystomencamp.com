Apply

Start the application

This is the first real step toward camp. It takes a few minutes, and our team will follow up personally with next steps, whether you are applying locally or from the diaspora.

Every location, Kampala, Arua, and Gulu, runs all four age bands. Choose whichever is closest to your family.

A 9-day intake in July, in Kampala, built specifically for boys and young men visiting from the diaspora.

Boys are placed into age-appropriate clusters automatically once they arrive, Young Explorers, Path Finders, Rising Men, or Young Men, based on age. There is nothing to choose here, every cluster is fully mentored and cared for.
Local intake fee: contact us for current pricing by region.

By submitting, you agree to be contacted by the Boys to Men team about this application.

What happens after you apply

1. Our team reviews your application and reaches out within a few working days.

2. We confirm intake dates, location, and cost, and answer any questions.

3. You complete registration and receive a full preparation guide before camp.

A calm, coaching-based approach

Boys to Men is not a disciplinary camp. Every mentor is vetted and supervised, and our approach is built on coaching and mentorship, not punishment. Read our full Safeguarding commitment.

Applying from the diaspora?

Choose "Diaspora Summer Camp" above so we can route your enquiry to the team handling travel logistics, timing, and communication across time zones.
