Safeguarding

Every boy in our care is protected, respected, and heard

Safeguarding is not a policy we keep in a drawer, it shapes how every session, every mentor interaction, and every camp day is run.

We are not a disciplinary camp

Boys to Men is a life readiness programme, not a bootcamp and not a disciplinary or correctional programme. There is no shouting, no punishment-based structure, and no military-style approach. Our results come from coaching, training, and mentorship, delivered through a calm, respectful approach.

Every mentor is vetted and supervised

No mentor works with boys unsupervised or unknown to the team. Every mentor is known personally to the Boys to Men leadership and supervised throughout every session.

Our Commitments

What safeguarding looks like day to day

A

Age-Appropriate Clustering

Boys are grouped by age automatically on arrival.

C

Open Communication

Parents are kept informed before, during, and after camp.

R

A Calm Environment

Mentors coach, not command. Boys are never shamed or singled out.

B

Clear Mentor Boundaries

Every mentor agrees to clear conduct expectations before working with any boy.

S

Safe Physical Environments

Venues and activities are chosen with physical safety as a non-negotiable baseline.

R

A Real Reporting Path

Any concern reaches the leadership team directly and is addressed promptly.

Have a safeguarding question or concern?

Speak to us directly. Every concern is taken seriously.
