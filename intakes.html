Intakes

Four age clusters, three regions, three windows a year

Boys to Men Mentorship Camp serves boys and young men from 8 to 23. Every location runs the full set of age clusters, in December and January, with a dedicated Summer Camp for diaspora families every July.

Age Clusters

Boys are clustered automatically, by age

There is nothing for a parent to choose here. Once boys arrive, they are placed into the cluster that fits their age, so every activity and conversation is pitched right.

Age BandNameFocus
23–18Young MenLife readiness, independence, and deploying what has been developed
17–14Rising MenIdentity, confidence, and early leadership development
13–11Path FindersBuilding the foundation between discovery and development
10–8Young ExplorersEarly self-discovery and self-awareness
