The men behind the mentorship

Every mentor is known to the team, vetted, and supervised throughout camp. Here is a first look at who your son will learn alongside.

Banio Luiji Nobert

Founder & Lead Mentor

Corporate trainer, coach, and author of twelve books, with over a decade mentoring individuals and teams across Uganda and Africa.

Mentor, Arua Region
