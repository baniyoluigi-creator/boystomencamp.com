About & Founder

Banio Luiji Nobert

Founder of Boys to Men Mentorship Camp and Team Leader of Potential Unexplored Ltd.

Our Story

Why Boys to Men exists

Boys to Men Mentorship Camp began as a response to a simple, urgent observation: too many boys are moving into manhood without the mentorship, guidance, or safe space they need to do it well. Banio Luiji Nobert, a corporate trainer, conference speaker, coach, and author of twelve books, brought over a decade of experience developing leaders and teams across Uganda and Africa to build a programme specifically for boys and young men.

The camp has since run multiple intakes across Kampala, Arua, and Gulu, and continues to grow alongside its sister programmes, Girls Rising and the MEP Masterclass, under the Potential Unexplored Ltd Life Readiness Programme.

Connection to PUL

A Potential Unexplored Ltd Initiative

Potential Unexplored Ltd believes every individual and organisation carries potential that remains unexplored until it is deliberately discovered, developed, and deployed. Boys to Men Mentorship Camp applies this same philosophy specifically to the journey from boyhood to manhood.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of responsible, confident, and purpose-driven men who positively influence their families, communities, and society.

Our Mission

To provide transformational mentorship experiences that help boys discover their potential, develop their capabilities, and deploy their strengths for meaningful impact.

Track Record

A decade of training leaders and teams across Africa

Banio's client portfolio includes UNHCR, Bank of Uganda, Centenary Bank, War Child Canada, CORDAID South Sudan, Mastercard Foundation, horizont3000, Opportunity International, and more than fifty institutions in total. He founded the MEP Masterclass, now past nineteen intakes, and coordinated the Enabling Rural Innovation Programme across East Africa. He is also the founder of GUWNSAA, the Gulu University West Nile Students Alumni Association.

Boys to Men Mentorship Camp is a flagship youth development initiative of Potential Unexplored Ltd, building bridges to manhood across Uganda and Africa.
