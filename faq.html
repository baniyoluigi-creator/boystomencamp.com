Apply

Start the application

This is the first real step toward camp. It takes a few minutes, and our team will follow up personally with next steps, whether you are applying locally or from the diaspora.

By submitting, you agree to be contacted by the Boys to Men team about this application.

What happens after you apply

1. Our team reviews your application and reaches out within a few working days.

2. We confirm intake dates, location, and cost, and answer any questions.

3. You complete registration and receive a full preparation guide before camp.

Applying from the diaspora?

Select "Diaspora" above so we can route your enquiry to the team handling travel logistics, timing, and communication across time zones.
