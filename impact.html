Beyond the Camp

What camp cannot finish in a few days

Camp opens the door. Beyond the Camp is both a documentary series and a real follow-up phase, built to close the gaps camp alone cannot.

Identity Started, Not Finished

A few days of self-discovery opens real questions. Beyond the Camp continues that conversation through ongoing mentor check-ins.

Skills Introduced, Not Practiced

What is learned at camp has to be tested in real life. Alumni community and accountability structures make that practice possible.

Mentorship Begun, Not Sustained

A mentor relationship built in a few days needs to continue. Follow-up contact keeps that relationship alive.
